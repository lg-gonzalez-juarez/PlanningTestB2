# Prepositions of time

## in:

- the morning 
- the afternoon
- the evening 
- December
- the summer
- 1998

## on:

- Monday (morning)
- the 12 th of July
- my birthday

## at:

- three o'clock
- midday/ midnight
- lunchtime
- the weekend
- christmas / Easter / New Year

Use in for parts of the day, months, seasons and years
Use on for days and dates
Use at for times of the day, night, the weekend and festivals
